Licensing

ETIketta D.C. works with official licenses for physical reissues on replicated CD. We are open to royalty-based agreements (typically a % of wholesale on units sold).

If you control a catalog (label, artist, estate, publisher), please include:

  • Title, year, original label
  • Confirmation of master ownership (recording rights)
  • Available sources (hi-res files / archive transfers / tape transfer options)
  • Territory and preferred quantities
