Label Mission

ETIchetta discoop is a boutique reissue label focused on overlooked gems: Italian library music, French jazz-funk, krautrock, prog, and strange cinematic artifacts.

Format: Pressed CD (replicated) — small, high-quality runs.

Approach: licensing, archival research, clean artwork, liner notes.

Goal: make rare music easy to own again.

Licensing & Rights

We license recordings for physical reissue on replicated CD. We can work on:

  • Royalty-based agreements (typically % of wholesale on units sold)
  • Limited pressings (e.g., 500–1500 units) with renewal options
  • Proper composer/publisher declarations via collecting societies

If you control a catalog (label, artist, estate, publisher) and want a high-quality reissue, contact us with: title, year, ownership confirmation, and available sources (digital masters, tapes, etc.).
